About Us

The Rasayan Club organizes events such as group discussions, scientific illustrations, quizzes, and poster competitions to evoke chemistry students' inclination towards addressing environmental and energy issues. These events aim to translate innovative ideas for a sustainable future and improve scientific reasoning among emerging researchers.

Our objective is to make students realize that chemical sciences can significantly contribute to solving societal problems.
